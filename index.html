Ghost Protocol

The system that protects you
when you can't protect yourself.

Most people don't realize they need help until it's too late to ask for it. Ghost Protocol runs silently in the background every hour of every day — watching for the moment you go quiet. When it comes, it doesn't wait.

18hrs

A personal check-in

Vera texts you directly. A real check-in, not an automated ping — making sure you're okay.

30hrs

The system escalates

Every agent is alerted. A flag goes up across your entire system and active escalation begins.

48hrs

Your people are reached

Your emergency contacts are called, texted, and emailed — with a full explanation of who Vera is, what Kore is, and exactly what's happening. No one is left confused or without a plan.

72hrs

Legal protection activates

If an arrest is detected in public records, your attorney receives a call, a text, and a complete legal briefing — your active case, court dates, outstanding fees, everything they need to act immediately.

AND IF YOU'RE ARRESTED?

You don't have to wait. Say "I'm being arrested" — Shield fires instantly. Attorney notified. Emergency contact notified. Bail monitoring starts. All within five minutes. The moment bail is set, everyone finds out.

If you can't speak for yourself — Vera speaks for you.

The Dead Man's Switch

For the people
you'd leave behind.

This is the feature nobody wants to think about. But everybody should have. If someone confirms you've passed, Kore doesn't go dark. It responds with humanity — like something that knew you.

  • Your loved ones receive your personal message — in your words, the things you wanted them to hear.

  • All wellness alerts and scheduled calls stop permanently. Your people aren't bombarded during the hardest moment of their lives.
  • Vera stays available — your family can reach it whenever they need to. To ask questions, to feel connected, to remember.
  • Over time, having learned your voice and your stories, Vera speaks about you to the people who loved you. Not pretending to be you. Representing you.
