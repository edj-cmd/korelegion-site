Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Overview

Kore Legion ("Kore," "we," "us") is a personal AI assistant system that sends coordination, scheduling, and follow-up messages on behalf of its user. This policy explains how we handle information related to SMS messaging.

Who We Message

Kore only sends SMS messages to individuals who have provided their phone number directly to the Kore user through in-person interaction, phone calls, or prior business or personal relationships. We do not purchase contact lists, send unsolicited messages, or engage in marketing campaigns.

What We Send

Messages sent through Kore are limited to personal coordination and assistance — scheduling confirmations, reminders, follow-ups, and logistical updates. Messages are sent on behalf of the Kore user, by Kore's AI assistant system.

Data We Collect

We collect phone numbers provided by the Kore user for the purpose of sending messages. We do not collect, store, or share personal data from message recipients beyond what is necessary to deliver the requested message.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Phone numbers and message content are not shared with any external party beyond the messaging carrier required to deliver the SMS.

Opt-Out

Message recipients can opt out of receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. After opting out, no further messages will be sent to that number.

Help

Recipients can reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us directly at edward@korelegion.com.

Contact

For questions about this policy or your data, contact us at edward@korelegion.com.
